9 – Days Northern Circuit via Lemosho Route

Descend through Mweka Gate.

Itinerary:

D00:24 August – Pickup Kilimanjaro Airport transfer to Moshi, overnight Weruweru Lodge
D00:24 August - Briefing/Equipment check overnight at Weruweru River Lodge.

D01:25 August – Day trip Safari. Morning transfer to Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive with

Picnic lunch, then late afternoon Transfer back to Moshi.


D02:26 Aug – Trek Lemosho Glades to Big Tree camp
D03:27 Aug – Trek Big Tree to Shira camp 01
D04:28 Aug – Trek Shira camp 01 to Shira camp 02

D05:29 Aug – Trek Shira camp 02 to Moir Huts camp (with acclimatization)
D06:30 Aug – Trek Moir Huts camp to Pofu Camp

D07:31 Aug – Trek Pofu Camp to Third Cave Camp

D08:01 Sept – Trek Third Cave Camp to School Hut Camp
D09:02 Sept – Trek School Hut to UHURU PEAK to Millennium Camp
D10:03 Sept – Trek Millennium Camp to Mweka Gate to the Weruweru River Lodge, Moshi


D11:04 Sept – Drop-off Kilimanjaro Airport for your flight home.
